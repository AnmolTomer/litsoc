About Us

The English Literary Association of VIT Chennai, or LitSoc, as we fondly refer to it, is a student club that was established in 2012 with the aim of congregating students with a shared love for literature. LitSoc is a safe space, where we nurture innovative and proactive ideas. Our teamwork and creativity are what sets us apart from the other chapters here at VIT Chennai. We always seek to do the best we can, regardless of how little or insignificant a task may seem.
